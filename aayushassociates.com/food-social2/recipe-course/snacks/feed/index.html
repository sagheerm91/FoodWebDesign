I lean way into the love them camp – in all varieties. Pickled anything makes me happy, and now is the time of year when you can really explore. Carrots, radishes, beets. I pickle a lot of things, but there’s nothing better than the classic. While you can add seasonings and mix up the styling of quick pickles, the key for me is starting with a 3-ingredient brine – equal parts water and white vinegar, plus kosher salt – to create the pickling liquid. The ratios are easily doubled or tripled for a larger batch. This one-cup to one-cup mix will generally create enough brine to fill at least three quart jars that have been tightly packed with veggies.
