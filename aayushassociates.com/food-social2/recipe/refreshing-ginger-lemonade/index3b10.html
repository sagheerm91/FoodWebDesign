Hot Honey Whipped Feta With Spicy Maple Almonds (EASY!)

It's getting hot in here! Try this easy hot honey-whipped feta dip made in a food processor. Easy clean up and extra rewarding to eat!

Cooking Method Broiling, Simmering
Courses Appetizer
Difficulty Beginner
Time
Prep Time: 8 min
Servings 6
Best Season Suitable throughout the year
Description
It's getting hot in here! Try this easy hot honey-whipped feta dip made in a food processor. Easy clean up and extra rewarding to eat!
 
Ingredients
    Spicy Maple Almonds
  • 1/2 cup Roasted Almonds
  • 1/4 cup Spicy Maple Syrup, (I used Zoë Olive Oil brand)
  • 3 tablespoons Granulated White Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground Cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground Nutmeg
    • Whipped Feta
  • 8 ounces Feta Cheese
  • 3 cloves cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1/3 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/4 cup Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 cup Hot Honey (Zoë Olive Oil)
  • 5 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil, (I used Zoë Olive Oil)
Process
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Line a baking dish with parchment paper and add your toasted almonds to the dish
  3. Coat the almonds with cinnamon, nutmeg, sugar, and maple syrup
  4. Bake for 12 minutes or until the maple syrup begins to bubble
  5. Set aside and let cool completely
  6. Once cooled, crush the almonds in a Ziploc with the back of a spoon
  7. In a food processor, add your feta cheese, olive oil, sour cream, lemon juice, salt, pepper, crushed garlic, and honey
  8. Blend until smooth
  9. Scrape the walls of the robot and mix again
  10. Place the whipped feta in a bowl and top with red pepper flakes, spiced honey, and our delicious almonds. Serve and enjoy!
