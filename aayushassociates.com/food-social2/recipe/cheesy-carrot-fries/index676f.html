Cheesy Carrot Fries

Courses Side Dishes
Servings 4
Ingredients
  • 2-4 Carrots
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic Powder
Process
  1. Cut carrots into sticks.
  2. Coat with olive oil, garlic and cheese.
  3. Cook in airfryer on fries function for 10 minutes.
  4. Top with more cheese, and garnish with parsley.
